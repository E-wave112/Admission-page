GENERAL ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

1.All participants are required to sit for the current Unified Tertiary Matriculation Exanination(UTME) and pick Bowen as their First Choice of Institution.

All participants are required to have credits at SSCE,GCE,NECO,NABTEB,or approved equivalent in at least five(5) subjects, which must include English Language and four(4) other subjects as may be specified by each Faculty. The five credits should be obtained in not more than two sittings.

For Medicine and Surgery(MBBS),Nursing Science and Physiotherapy programmes, the five(5) credits (Including Mathematics and English Language) must be obtained at only one sitting.
